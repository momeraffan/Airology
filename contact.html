What is Airology?

Airology is a brand launched by 'Nadir Magsi' - a respected faculty member of Air University Multan - represents a platform that enables personal and professional growth of individuals through a series of well structured and organized sessions of workshops/seminars/informal-talks/discussions. The platform does not limit the scope of ones perspective by imposing a restriction by filtering out particular topics, instead, the platform aims to target all the possible subjects one might imagine worthy to be pondered upon.
